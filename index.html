How it works

Three simple steps to turn WhatsApp into a marketing engine.

1

Connect your session

Use whatsapp-web.js on your server to generate a QR code. WaveReach displays it and tracks session status for your team.

2

Import your audience

Upload .txt lists with one phone per line. Clean and validate numbers before you hit send.

3

Launch campaigns

Use templates, variables and pacing. The front-end stays simple, while your backend handles all the heavy lifting.
